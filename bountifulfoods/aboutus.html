About Us

Our Story and commitment

Our commitment as a company is to provide our customers in California with the best organic products available. We take great pride in offering top-quality items that you can trust to be safe, healthy, and sustainable. Let us help you make a positive impact on your health and the environment by choosing our organic products today.

Our goal was to encourage people to eat more fruits and vegetables. To achieve this, we chose to cultivate and distribute the produce from our own garden.

We began as a small local enterprise, and now, owing to its widespread popularity, we have establishments all along the coast of California.
