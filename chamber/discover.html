Victoria is a small town, but surrounded by beautiful places to travel. This area is well known by its volcanoes views and the great amount of natural reservation across the region.

The beautiful Victoria's plaza. It's placed in the center of the city, people meet here for diferent type of recreational activities, like music concerts, special holidays, fairs, etc...

Collipulli and it wonders, it's one of the most rainy places,which enhances the diversity of flora in the area. We can also find the "Viaducto del Malleco", a railway bridge built in October 26th, 1890. By the french company "Schneider & Cie de Le Creusot".

With a surface of 60.832 ha, The National Park of Conguillio, offers the wonderful view of the 3 principal volcanoes in the area. It's well known by the different type of landscape, from a dry and rocky view to a humid, moldy and flowery one.

The National Park of Tolhuaca, has a surface of 6.474 ha. Despite the smaller size, its mountainous structure makes it a place with an exquisite forest wealth. The principal flora we can find here are the coigues, lenga and araucaria trees.
